About us

Our Story

Our story begins down to earth, local irish farms where all our dairy and fruit is sourced!

Fresh Ingredients Ever

Our heavenly base is made from milk and cream, with all dairy supplied by 2,100 farmers across Ireland, where cows breate in the green pastures and fresh ambeint.

Health

We care about peoples health that's why we steer clear of vegetable and palm oils that could take from the creaminess of our Whipped Irish Ice Cream and use natural flavours as much as possible.

Mood

So, whether you have the Monday blues or you’re riding high on that Friday feeling, we’ll do our best to uplift your mood to that next level. Give us a whirl. Our unique ice cream cakes are just perfect for birthdays, communions, confirmations and other family celebrations.
